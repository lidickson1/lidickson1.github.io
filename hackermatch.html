Hackermatch

A Tinder like matching web application for hackers to easily form teams for hackathons. Built using Materialize, jQuery, NodeJS, Heroku and Firebase.

You can easily register an account, and then start by creating a profile and fill out your preferred technologies, custom keywords, and contact info.

Navigate to the "Matching" tab and start matching! Potential matches are shown based on a custom matching algorithm that has been implemented which accounts for tech expertise and interests. You can choose to match or pass on a potential match.

Once you have successfully matched with someone, you can view their contact info and introduce yourselves to each other. You can also choose to unmatch if the match is undesirable.
